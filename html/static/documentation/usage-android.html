S P A C E Android Usage

Account

Create Account

Create an account by accepting the legalese, choosing an alias and password, and tapping create. A new 4096-bit RSA key pair will be generated from which the public key will be registered to you alias with the network, and the private key will be encrypted with your password and stored on you device.

Unlock Account

Unlock your account by tapping on your alias and entering your password.

Import/Export Account

Export your account from one device and import to another using the key share server by tapping Account -> Export Keys.

Enter your password to unlock your keys

An access code is be generated and used to encrypt your keys and password before they are uploaded to the key share server.

On the second device tap Import Keys and enter your alias and the access code generated by the first device

You keys are downloaded from the key share server, decrypted with the access code and installed on your device

Note: The key share server will discard your keys after 2 minutes.

Delete Account

Delete your account by either long-pressing your alias on the AccessActivity, or tapping the "Delete Account" button at the bottom of the AccountActivity.

Note: Deleting your account will permanently delete the keys from your device, however it will not delete your data from the blockchain.

Settings

The Settings page holds options to customize the app to your taste and can be accessed from the drop-down menu on the Main page.

General

Sort Order

You documents can either be sorted chronologically (oldest first), or reverse-chronologically (newest first).

Security

Biometric Unlock

Your account can be unlocked using your fingerprint if supported by your device. This option is more convenient that entering your password each time.

Cache

Cache Size

Displays the current size of the cache.

Purge Cache

This option deletes all data from the cache.

About

App Version

Display the version of the app

Support

This option composes an email to support@aletheiaware.com containing information about your account, your device, and the app you are using to facilitate a quick resolution of your issue. The pre-composed email will be opened in your preferred email client for you to explain the issue before sending.
