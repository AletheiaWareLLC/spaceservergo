Mining Service

Aletheia Ware provides Mining Service on a metered rate of $0.05 per group of 1,000,000 bytes (1MB) per month up to a maximum of 2,000,000,000 bytes (2GB). The table below gives examples of the cost for different monthly usages.

Usage Cost
0 bytes $0.00
800KB $0.05
28MB $1.40
564MB $28.20
1.2GB $60.00

Registered customers using the Mining Service without a subscription will be charged on a per-use basis.

Note: If a subscribed customer's bill fails to meet Stripe's minimum charge amount the customer will not be billed and the amount due will be rolled over into the next month's bill. Alternatively, if an unsubscribed customer's bill fails to meet Stripe's minimum charge amount the customer will be billed the minimum charge amount.
