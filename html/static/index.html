Secure, Private, Storage.

When deciding how to store your personal notes, dog pictures, cat videos, music playlists, health records, bank statements, government documents, and other aspects of your digital life, consider the following three aspects;

  • Availability - is your data available where and when you need it?
  • Confidentiality - is your data accessible only to you?
  • Integrity - can your data be modified without your authorization or knowledge?

For example; external storage drives are cheaper than ever and can store huge amounts of data. However, you need to carry them around with you and if they get damaged, lost, or stolen your data is gone. Alternatively, cloud storage allows you to access your data from anywhere with an internet connection, but the host is able to read and modify your data without your knowledge.

Isn't there a better way? Yes - indelible, immutable, private, and secure digital storage using blockchains!

Space encrypts your data and mines it into a blockchain ensuring: availability - your encrypted data is stored on a network spanning the globe and can be cached locally for offline access; confidentiality - your data can only be read after it is decrypted with a private key only you hold; integrity - a cryptographic signature of your encrypted data, and a cryptographic hash of the block it is mined into, ensure your data is indelible and immutable.
