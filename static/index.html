Space

Welcome to Space, please follow the steps below to get setup;

  1. Download Space for your device;

    • Android - Download
    • iOS - Download
    • Linux - Download
    • Mac - Download
    • Windows - Download

  2. Generate your key pair consisting of a public key (which will be registered with your account) and a private key (which does not leave your possession).

    $> ./space
    Creating keystore under /Users/username/bc
    Enter keystore password:
    Confirm keystore password:
    Generating RSA 4096 Public/Private Key Pair
    Successfully created keystore!

    Register your public key and billing information at https://space.aletheiaware.com/register

    -----BEGIN RSA PUBLIC KEY-----
    ...
    ...
    -----END RSA PUBLIC KEY-----

  3. Follow the URL given by Space which will return you to this website to complete your registration and provide the following information;

    • Alias - what you would like us to call you.
    • Email - how you would like us to contact you.
    • Billing Info - how you would like to pay (this information is handled and stored by Stripe);
      • Card Holder Name
      • Card Number
      • Card Expiry Date
      • Card Verification Code (CVC)
      • Billing Address
